Guideline

There are 4 options: Play alone, Play with AI, Play in room, and Set up the board

  1. Play alone: Users press on the button "PLAY ALONE" on the front page and practice alone.
  2. Play with AI: Users play directly on the front page. There are 5 levels: Newbie, Easy, Normal, Hard, and Hardest.
  3. Play in room: Users press on the button "START", host a new room with random Room code, and create a password for you and your friend, also capable of Inviting friend to play by sending the link. Users can also access the page "Rooms" to enter a hosted room. Users can choose White Side or Black Side, White moves first.
  4. Set up the board: Players press on the link "Set Up". In this option, players can arrange the chess pieces and press "CAPTURE THE BOARD" to challenge friends.
